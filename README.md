# ⚔️ Atlas Custom Enchants

**Atlas Custom Enchants** is a premium-tier Minecraft enchantment plugin originally developed for a private Survival Multiplayer (SMP) server. It bridges the gap between vanilla gameplay and high-tier RPG progression, offering players unique mechanics and long-term objectives.

---

## 🚀 Core Features

* **🧰 Fully Configurable:** Every enchantment, rarity, value, and message can be tuned to fit your server's specific economy.
* **⚡ Performance First:** Built from the ground up to be lightweight. It won't impact your TPS, even during heavy combat or large-scale mining.
* **🎲 Tiered Rarity System:** Six distinct tiers (**Divine** to **Trash**) create a natural sense of progression and excitement during enchanting.
* **🧪 Advanced Systems:** Includes the **Oracle of Enchantment** and the **Altar of Circe** for an interactive gameplay experience beyond simple menus.
* **💬 Custom Branding:** All plugin messages are customizable to match your server’s unique aesthetic and language.

---

## 💎 The Enchantment Tiers

### ✨ Divine
* **Poseidon's Bait** ➪ A mystical fishing enchantment that significantly enhances rewards from the deep.

### 🔱 Godly
* **Fearsight** ➪ Visual awareness: Aggressive mobs glow **Red**, Players/Villagers glow **White**, and Passives glow **Green**.
* **Blessing of Knowledge** ➪ Displays a health bar above any mob's head.
* **Miner's Touch** ➪ The ultimate utility. Allows you to mine and collect Mob Spawners.
* **Asclepius** ➪ Grants the wearer additional permanent heart containers.

### 🎖️ Legendary
* **Leech** ➪ Converts a portion of damage dealt into health for the user.
* **Safe Miner** ➪ Teleports mined items directly into your inventory.
* **Extractor** ➪ Greatly increases the amount of EXP dropped from kills.
* **Vein Seeker** ➪ Chain-mines entire ore veins instantly.
* **Wings of Aegis** ➪ Grants passive damage reduction against incoming attacks.
* **Final Guard** ➪ A protective aura that grants a chance to prevent item durability loss.

### 🌀 Epic
* **Rush** ➪ Provides a temporary Speed boost upon activation.
* **Stunning** ➪ Chance to temporarily immobilize hit entities.
* **Energy Absorption** ➪ Provides emergency hearts when the player drops below a health threshold.
* **Poison/Ice Aspect** ➪ Applies debilitating Poison or Frozen effects to enemies.
* **Tree Hugger** ➪ Chops down entire trees in a single swing.

### 🔹 Rare & Below
* **Rare:** Includes **Hunter** (Passive mob damage), **Propel** (Launch enemies), **Freezing Shot**, **Regrowth** (Auto-replant), and **Decapitate** (Head drops).
* **Trash:** **Tainted Book** A failed enchantment result used in various crafting or recycling mechanics.

## 🛠️ Special Items & Systems

| Item / System | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Oblivion Shard** | A rare material used for high-level enchantment manipulation. |
| **Scrap of Circe Weave** | A crafting component essential for upgrading your gear. |
| **Oracle of Enchantment** | The primary system used to reveal or upgrade hidden enchantments. |
| **Altar of Circe** | A workstation where players can manipulate enchants using special items. |

## ⚙️ Configuration
You have total control over the plugin's behavior. Inside the configuration files, you can modify:
* **Enchantment Stats:** Levels, proc chances, and rarity distribution.
* **Visuals:** GUI layouts, item materials, and particle effects.
* **Logic:** Custom internal names, experience costs, and item requirements.
